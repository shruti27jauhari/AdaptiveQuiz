Instructions

Welcome to our platform! Before proceeding, please read and understand the following guidelines:

  1. Make sure mobile/laptop is fully charged. Power bank for mobile or UPS/Inverter for laptop/desktop should be arranged for uninterrupted power supply.
  2. Students should have sufficient data in Fair Usage Policy (FUP) / Internet plan with sufficient data pack of internet service provider.
  3. Login to the portal 10 min before the online examination start time.
  4. Do Not Pickup/Receive the Call during the exam if you are giving the exam on mobile. This also will be treated as changing the window.
  5. To avoid unwanted pop-ups, use of Ad Blocker is recommended.

General Instructions

  1. This is an online exam that consists of multiple-choice questions.
  2. Please read each question carefully before selecting your answer.
  3. Ensure you have a stable internet connection throughout the exam.
  4. The timer on the top right corner shows the remaining time for the exam.

Exam Specifics

  1. You will have a total of 60 minutes to complete the exam.
  2. The exam consists of 50 multiple-choice questions.
  3. Each question has a single correct answer.
  4. Click the "Submit" button when you have answered all questions or when time is up.
